Introduction

Turkey 365

A modern approach to the food of the famous American holiday. Turkey 365 serves the delicious meal of thanksgiving all year long.

Target audience

Turkey 365 serves upper-middle class Americans who are looking for modern meals that both taste like home and are made from fresh, healthy ingredients. Millenials, in paticular, are a target audience because of the unique and trendy nature of Turkey 365. What millenial wouldn't want to try a modern spin on the comforting thanksgiving meal they so happily reminisce about. Nonetheless, Turkey 365 serves food that can be enjoyed by people of all ages, including young children and senior cxitizens alike.

Turkey 365 hopes to serve the comfortable, familiar and tasty flavors of thanksgiving to anyone seeking a "home cooked meal" without the hassle of shopping for ingredients,cooking the meal and cleaning the kitchen. Turkey 365 wants its customers to feel at home.

About

A dining experience like no other -- serving the food you crave 364 days of the year. Why not enjoy a meal of golden brown, juicy turkey, creamy heavenly mashed potatoes and a side of flavorful, blistering grean beans any day you'd like? The glorious turkey meal we serve and eat should not be limited to one evening in late November. At Turkey 365, we believe the magical meal of thanksgiving deserves more than just one day. Come enjoy a tasty, homestyle meal with all your favorites - smoked turkey, creamy mashed potatoes, crispy crunchy green beans and don't forget the gravy!
